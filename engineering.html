The platform

Built like equipment, not a toy.

A purpose-built, custom Klipper-controlled CAN-bus machine — engineered for high-temperature engineering polymers and abrasive composites, tuned for speed without sacrificing precision.

Chamber

Fully enclosed

Heated, stable chamber for warp-prone high-temp materials like ABS, ASA and PC.

Hotend

High-flow, all-metal

350 °C-class hotend with a CF-ready all-metal heat break — fast throughput, demanding materials.

Nozzles

Hardened & CHT

Wear-resistant hardened and high-flow nozzles for abrasive carbon-fibre composites.

Motion

Input-shaped

CAN-bus toolhead with onboard accelerometer and resonance compensation for clean, fast travel.

Envelope

Large format

Generous build volume for sizeable functional parts and multi-part batches in a single run.

Probing

Auto-levelled

Automated bed mapping for a dependable first layer across the whole plate, print after print.
