Selected work

Designed, printed, produced.

A custom motion platform engineered from scratch, a discontinued part brought back, and a brand mascot made real — a cross-section of what FlowForge designs and prints, from one-off engineering to bespoke studio pieces.

Design · CAD

A machine, designed from the ground up

Not a kit and not a mod — a custom CoreXY motion platform modelled entirely in CAD, from the central spider plates to every corner bracket and the belt path between them. Each part is designed to be made, fitted and serviced. It's the clearest demonstration of our design-and-engineer capability: bring us a problem and we'll design the whole solution, part by part.

Engineering · Replacement

A discontinued part, recreated

A carburettor cover for an early-1980s motorcycle — long out of production and impossible to buy. Working from the original, we reverse-engineered the geometry in CAD, outer form, internal structure and mounting points, and reprinted it as a clean, fitted replacement. The part you can't get anymore is exactly the kind of job we're built for.

Studio · Bespoke

A 2D mascot, made real

A brand mascot, engineered from a 2D logo into a fully functional 3D jar — design, print and finish in one piece. We modelled the character, the body and the threaded screw lid from a client's flat artwork, then printed and finished it as a working container. Detailed, fiddly, and exactly the kind of bespoke build the Studio side exists for.
